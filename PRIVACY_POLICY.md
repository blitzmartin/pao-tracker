# Privacy Policy for PAO Tracker

## Introduction

PAO Tracker ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using PAO Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

PAO Tracker does not collect any personally identifiable information about you. We do not require account creation, registration, or any form of user authentication.

### App Data

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

- Beauty product names
- Product categories (e.g., Skincare, Makeup, Hair Care)
- Expiry dates
- Opening dates (for PAO - Period After Opening calculations)
- PAO duration in months
- Date format preferences
- Notification settings

All data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## How We Use Information

We use the stored information solely to provide the App's core functionality:

- Track beauty product expiry dates
- Calculate Period After Opening (PAO) expiry dates
- Display product information in the app interface
- Send local notifications for upcoming product expiries
- Maintain user preferences (date format, notification settings)

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

PAO Tracker does not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any information stored in the App to any third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no opportunity for external sharing.

## Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. All data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any external entities. However, please note:

- Device security is your responsibility
- Data may be lost if your device is damaged, lost, or reset
- We recommend regularly backing up important data

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To send reminders about expiring beauty products. You can enable/disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

Permission to send notifications is optional and can be revoked through your device settings.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You have full control over your data
- You can delete individual products or clear all data through the app
- You can uninstall the app to permanently remove all stored data
- No data recovery is possible once deleted

## Children's Privacy

PAO Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
